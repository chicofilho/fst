Tahiti Pro Results

Tahiti was an amazing event with the King Kelly showing dominance over the championship.

The waves of Teahupoo are pretty predictable, it is a litle easier compared to other venues. But it is easy for everyone, so it's difficult to get high positions as most players chose great teams. There was only one single difference between the Machine and the Human, and it was Kai Otton vs. Bruno Santos. And that made the Human get the position 900 and the Machine 7.000 on the event's leaderboard.

The points were not so close, as Bruno Santos made lots of more points than Kai Otton.

The Fantasy Bot scored 577.63 out of 801.71 possible points. The Human team was able to score a total of 635.43 points. Check out the Bot and Specialists teams.
